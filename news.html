Band Represents Central Mountain at LL Grand Slam Parade

August 12, 2025

The Marching Wildcats traveled to Williamsport on Tuesday to perform in the Little League Parade. This parade is always a hit with the students.

The event is long and tiring, but the route is full of thousands of spectators and their performance is always televised near the end of the parade.

CMMB Performs The Star-Spangled Banner at Clinton County Speedway

August 1, 2025

On Friday, August 1, 2025, the band was invited to perform the National Anthem, starting off the evening at the Clinton County Speedway

This is the second year the band has been able to add this performance to their calendar, and they hope to continue in years to come. The students also pumped up the crowd beforehand with a band favorite, "Louie, Louie."

Band Camp: A Hot, Sweaty Success

July 25, 2025

Once again, the CM Marching Wildcats took to the field in the summer heat to learn their marching band field show. Working to memorize music, performing dance and equipment routines, and memorizing where 70 unique spots are on the field (and how to get there) are what band camp is all about.

The students and instructors pressed on early in the week to learn more outside work than usual, knowing the heat in the latter part of the week would be unbearable. The students had a good handle on the entire drill by the end of the day Wednesday, and will now work to clean things up and improve their performance each week. Congratulations on another successful band camp!
